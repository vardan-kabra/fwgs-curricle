Pillar 1

Your school email ID

Every family gets a school email address in this format:

p.firstname.lastname@fwgs.in

p. = parent · firstname.lastname = your child's name · fwgs.in = the school domain. Students in Grade 6 and above also receive a school email, with an s. prefix in place of p.

Enter your own age, not your child's.

When Google asks for your age at first login, enter your age — not your child's. Google automatically deactivates accounts it believes belong to someone under 13, and reactivating a deactivated account takes 45 days. This is the single most important step when you first sign in.

How we reach you

Two parallel hubs carry almost everything the school sends you — one academic, one administrative.

Academic

  • Academic updates & unit overviews

  • Materials, resources & curriculum

Administrative · iOS · Android · Web

Nucleus

  • Newsletters, PTC & term-end reports
  • Events, field trips & opportunities
  • Fees, transport & attendance
  • LC applications, grievances & forms

Inside Nucleus' Bulletin, every post is flagged so you know how to treat it:

MUST KNOW

Critical, time-sensitive, or compulsory. Don't miss these.

GOOD TO KNOW

Optional and informational — useful, but not urgent.

Official WhatsApp broadcast

The school runs whole-school and grade-wise broadcast lists for short nudges that link back here or to Nucleus. Save our number to receive them. Parent-created WhatsApp groups are not official channels.

Managing your inbox

Primary tab: Must-Know notices and 1:1 emails from teachers or leaders. Promotions: Good-to-Know updates. Classroom category: academic notifications. Treat any personal 1:1 email as a priority — we aim to reply within a working day, and ask the same of you.

Pillar 3

How you reach us

Pick the stream that matches your question, then start at the top of the list and work down.

Stream A — Admin

Transport, food, I-Card, uniform, fees

  1. Email info@fwgs.in Our primary admin channel — actively encouraged for all logistical queries.
  2. WhatsApp +91 96576 62888 Text preferred. Voice notes won't be addressed.
  3. Call +91 96576 62888 Last resort — not for routine queries; emergencies only.

Still stuck? Escalate to Kiran Kante (Admin Operations Manager) or Abhijit Dive (Head of Admin). Structured requests — LC, grievances, fees — move to Nucleus forms as those modules come online.

Stream B — Academic

Learning, progress, classroom matters

  1. Email your child's teacher firstname.lastname@fwgs.in — teachers are also reachable through Google Classroom.
  2. Escalate up the ladder Team Leader → Coordinator → Head of School (see the directory below).
  3. Something confidential? Write directly to the Team Leader, Coordinator, or Head of School.
